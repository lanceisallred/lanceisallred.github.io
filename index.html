Our Project

A short description of our HIIT workout app.

High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT), an intense workout method that is designed to increase cardiovascular health, centers around pushing the human body to maximum exhaustion in very short intervals. Our mobile application allows users to generate randomized high intensity workouts based on things like available equipment and which muscles they would like to exercise. Unobtrusive visual and auditory aid will accompany each exercise to ensure a user has proper form and is staying on time. The application offers a polished experience that allows users to exercise no matter their location, equipment on hand, or available time.

Users will be pushed to their limits in a variety of exercises such as push ups, squats, jumping jacks, and mountain climbers as our application takes them on a journey of temporal pain on the path to long term pleasure. Ultimately, we deliver a concise yet difficult road to our user's ultimate destination of functionally effective bodies honed in the abilities of natural movements. By removing the burden of users having to organize and track their own workouts, we are empowering them to work as effectively as possible and encouraging them to push themselves beyond the limits of what they're capable of.
